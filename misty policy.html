Misty Hotel Food and Beverage Department

Complaint Handling Policy and Procedures

The policy statement

The Food and Beverage department is committed to providing all customers with undifferentiated and inclusive services... enhance our competitiveness in the market.

The policy

1. Service standards for all staff

Communication attitude:

  • When receiving complaints, you must keep smiling... avoid using commanding tone.
  • For customers with different cultural backgrounds... the meals should be replaced.

All staff must abide by the principle of non-discriminatory service:

  • All customers should be treated equally... cultural background.
  • For different types of customers... written rectification report for enterprise customers, etc.
  • Multicultural service training... before they can work.

Code of Business Ethics:

  • When handling complaints, employees are strictly prohibited... sensitive internal information of the hotel.
  • The investigation and handling of complaints should be objective and neutral... any side should be favored.

Food Safety and Labeling Standards:

Ensure that the food provided meets safety and hygiene standards... allergens, etc.

Customer rights protection:

For reasonable refund or exchange requests from customers... safeguard the legitimate rights and interests of consumers.

Forced norms of special customer group services:

  • People with disabilities: actively provide voice guidance... barrier-free areas.
  • Pregnant women / customers with children: adjust the dining environment... when children complain.
  • Multicultural customer groups: When Muslim customers complain... ingredients meet Jewish rules.

2. Customer feedback is welcome:

QR code for complaints and opinion book are placed... give feedback at any time.

  • All complaints should be entered into the system... facilitate subsequent service optimization.
  • All complaints should be responded to within 24 hours... Complex complaints should not be closed within 24 hours.
  • The complaint rate needs to be reduced by more than 30%... customer satisfaction should reach more than 90%.
  • Complaints need to be translated into service improvement opportunities... optimize the process.

Hard indicators of response timeliness:

  • On-site complaint: The waiter will notify the manager within 5 minutes, and the manager will arrive within 10 minutes.
  • Online complaints: Initial response within 4 hours, closed loop within 24 hours.
  • Telephone complaints: Answer within 3 rings. For complex issues, update the progress every 2 hours.

Policy Implementation:

Employee Training:

  • Organize monthly training on complaint handling... policy procedures.
  • Strengthen employees' coping ability... through role-playing.
  • New employees need to complete special complaint handling training before they can start work.

Supervision and Evaluation:

  • Set up full-time complaint handling supervisors... implement the policy.
  • Collect and report the monthly complaint data... to the restaurant manager.

reward and punishment mechanism:

  • Outstanding performers... promotion opportunities and other rewards.
  • If the customer is dissatisfied due to violation... punishment will be imposed according to the circumstances.

Communication and Feedback:

  • Within 24 hours after the complaint is submitted... confirm the importance of the feedback.
  • After the completion of the processing... actively ask for subsequent requirements.
  • Collect feedback through online questionnaires... optimize policies.

Complaint Handling Procedure

1. Scope and purpose

Scope: This program is applicable to the acceptance... environmental health.

Purpose: Standardize the complaint handling process... operational management.

2. Definitions

  • Simple complaints: problems that can be solved on the spot... less than 30 minutes.
  • Complex complaints: issues involving multi-department coordination... > 30 minutes.

3. Responsibilities

OccupationResponsibility description
Worker at the production lineRecord the complaint information in time... provide necessary support.
Food and beverage managerArrive at the scene within 10 minutes... supervise the progress of handling.

4. Procedure

4.1 Complaint acceptance

Complaint channels: On-site, online, telephone/email.

Record requirements: Fill in Complaint Registration Form and synchronize to manager.

4.2 On-site response

  • Within 5 minutes: Waiter informs manager and calms customer.
  • Within 10 minutes: Manager arrives, apologizes, proposes temporary solutions.

4.3 Classified process

Type of complaintProcessing procedureTime limit
Simple complaintsMake on-site solutions... confirm satisfaction.≤ 30 min
Complex complaintsHold cross-departmental meetings... update progress every 2 hours.≤ 24 hours
